New to the Neighborhood?
Turning 16* Soon?
Be Sure to Register to Vote!

*You can register at 16 to vote at 18, and if you’ll be 18 in time for the General Election you can vote in that Primary.

Online at:
voterservices.elections.maryland.gov/ OnlineVoterRegistration/InstructionsStep1

Download an application at:
www.montgomerycountymd.gov/Elections/ registration/VoterRegistration.html

Call the 24-hour voter information line at 240-777-VOTE and leave your name and address after the recorded message.

For more information visit www.montgomerycountymd.gov/elections
